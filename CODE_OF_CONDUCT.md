# Code of Conduct

Marmot is a small open-source project and welcomes practical, respectful
collaboration. Participants must avoid harassment, discrimination, threats,
personal attacks, doxxing, and deliberate exposure of private data or
credentials.

Keep reviews focused on the work, explain disagreement with evidence, and make
space for contributors with different devices, languages, and levels of
experience. Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation
when needed to protect the community.

For urgent safety or security concerns, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
