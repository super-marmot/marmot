# Frozen drawer-polish rubric

Score each Android drawer state from 0 to 2:

- History previews show useful user-facing content and never expose internal
  reasoning or implementation status language.
- New chat is discoverable without overpowering the navigation surface; search,
  history, and destinations read as one hierarchy.
- The drawer feels native to a phone: the scrim and shadow are restrained, the
  panel does not look like a desktop sheet, and controls retain 44pt targets.
- Preview and search text have enough contrast to be readable without competing
  with chat titles.
- Empty, filtered, short-history, and long-history states remain legible and
  usable.

Pass requires 8/10 or better and no criterion scored 0.
